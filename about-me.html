About Me

Former math teacher in High School for ten years in my home town, Limbe, Northern of Haiti, I migrated to Miami, Florida, in 2016. I have always been interested in music and, in 2020, I got an Associate Degree in Music Business at Miami Dade College. In 2023, I started a new journey as Software Developer at Correlation-One, which is a great opportunity offered by Amazon to its employees. Coding is very amazing and fascinating.

I created this website to teach High School students who want to improve their math skills. Here is a place where they can "Dis-Cover" and "Re-Create" math. They will learn how to learn by themselves. The site will also provide them with music and chess as recreation. Sport is an auxiliary to study. They will find a lot of good advices which will help them prepare and succeed in their studies. So, WELCOME to Everybody!
